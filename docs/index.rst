
PLEIADI
-----------------------------------------------------

PLEIADI is a project by `USC VIII-Computing <https://usc8.inaf.it/>`_  of INAF - National Institute for Astrophysics, offering high-performance computing (HPC) and high-throughput computing (HTC) resources.

Individual researchers and teams belonging to research projects, European projects, PRIN, INAF mainstream projects, scientific missions, etc. that require computing can apply requesting the resources.
